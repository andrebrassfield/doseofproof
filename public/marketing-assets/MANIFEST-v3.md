## Usage Guide

### X/Twitter Strategy

1. **Thread Starters** — Use thread cover images to hook viewers into long-form content
2. **Daily Posts** — Mix proof, contrarian, educational, and personal story posts
3. **Pinned Tweet** — Pin the data-driven image to your profile for credibility
4. **Engagement** — Post 3-5x per week, rotating through different post types

### LinkedIn Strategy

1. **Professional Authority** — Use explainer and deep-dive images for thought leadership
2. **Connection Focus** — Share the mold-CCI and terrain model posts for professional audience
3. **Compound Education** — Post individual peptide/compound deep-dives weekly
4. **Cadence** — 2-3 posts per week for consistent visibility

### Instagram Strategy

1. **Carousel Posts** — Use carousel covers to drive engagement and swipe-throughs
2. **Quote/Proof Cards** — Post standalone cards 2-3x per week for daily engagement
3. **Stories** — Use story covers to tease carousel content and drive traffic
4. **Highlights** — Organize highlights by topic (Mold, Peptides, CCI, etc.) for easy navigation
5. **Reels** — Use story covers as thumbnail/cover images for video content

### TikTok Strategy

1. **Video Thumbnails** — Use these images as cover frames for your TikTok videos
2. **Hook Optimization** — Each thumbnail is designed to stop the scroll
3. **Vertical Format** — All images are optimized for 9:16 vertical viewing
4. **Trending Angles** — Mix before/after, educational, and contrarian content

## Implementation Tips

### Best Practices

1. **Consistency** — Use the same color palette and style across all platforms
2. **Scheduling** — Plan 2-4 weeks of content in advance using a content calendar
3. **Rotation** — Cycle through different post types to maintain audience interest
4. **Testing** — A/B test different hooks and messaging to find what resonates
5. **Timing** — Post when your audience is most active (typically 8-10am, 12pm, 6-8pm)

### Platform-Specific Tips

**X/Twitter:**
- Thread starters perform best on weekday mornings
- Proof posts get high engagement from health/biotech audiences
- Contrarian posts drive retweets and discussion
- Pin your best-performing post to your profile

**LinkedIn:**
- Professional tone resonates with healthcare and tech audiences
- Educational posts get more engagement than promotional content
- Share personal stories to build credibility
- Use relevant hashtags (#health #biohacking #peptides #protocol)

**Instagram:**
- Carousel posts get 3x more engagement than single posts
- Stories drive traffic to your main feed
- Highlights help new followers understand your content
- Use relevant hashtags and location tags

**TikTok:**
- Hook viewers in the first 3 seconds
- Use trending sounds and transitions
- Post consistently (3-5x per week minimum)
- Engage with comments to boost algorithmic reach
