## v2.1 — Drainage-first sequencing (2026-05-15)

The biggest unlock in this version was committing to a hard 4-week drainage phase before any inflammatory modulation. Binders were still in the stack, but binders without drainage were producing die-off that masqueraded as Herxheimer reactions.

### What changed

- **Drainage first.** Liposomal glutathione + phosphatidylcholine AM, binder blend (activated charcoal + chlorella + bentonite) PM, with daily Epsom salt baths.
- **DAO at every meal.** Diamine oxidase enzyme to clear dietary histamine before it crosses the gut wall.
- **NUCCA cadence reduced.** Once alignment held, weekly adjustments were producing over-treatment symptoms. Three-week cadence matched the recovery curve.

### What I tracked

The most honest single metric was the HRV baseline. The full trace is in the dashboard view; the headline is the jump from 14 ms (chronic subluxation state) to 48 ms (post-NUCCA aligned). That's a 3.4× improvement and the first time the number crossed the "30 ms = vagal tone reactivated" threshold my NUCCA specialist watches for.

### What's still broken

- Morning sinus pressure (down 60% but not gone)
- Post-exertional fatigue (12-18 hour delay after moderate cardio)
- Cold extremities (rewarming time still 2× baseline)

These carry into v2.2.
