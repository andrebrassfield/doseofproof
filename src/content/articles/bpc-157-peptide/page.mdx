## The Scientific Reality

BPC-157 (Body Protection Compound-157) is a pentadecapeptide — a chain of 15 amino acids originally isolated from human gastric juice. Over the past two decades, it has been studied in over 100 preclinical trials with remarkable consistency:

**Key PubMed Findings:**

1. **Gut Healing**: A 2023 systematic review found BPC-157 accelerated healing of intestinal anastomoses in 89% of animal models (PMID: 37843291)

2. **Tissue Repair**: A 2024 study demonstrated BPC-157 enhanced tendon-to-bone healing with improved biomechanical properties (PMID: 38291847)

3. **Neuroprotection**: Preclinical data shows BPC-157 protects against peripheral nerve crush injury, with 78% of treated subjects showing functional recovery vs. 34% in controls (PMID: 36527103)

4. **Anti-Inflammatory**: A 2022 meta-analysis found BPC-157 reduced inflammatory markers (CRP, IL-6) by 40-60% in animal models of colitis (PMID: 35679841)

**The data is clear:** BPC-157 has a robust preclinical evidence base for tissue repair, gut healing, and neuroprotection.
