# The 6 Tests They Missed

Standard medicine looks for extreme pathology. If you aren't dying right now, you're "fine." But chronic illness lives in the optimal vs. sub-optimal gray area.

1. **Urine Mycotoxin Panel:** To prove the mold is actually in your system.
2. **Upright Cervical MRI / DMX:** To catch the mechanical instability of CCI.
3. **Organic Acids Test (OAT):** To see cellular energy production and fungal overgrowth.
4. **Comprehensive Stool Analysis (GI Map):** To check the microbiome and leaky gut markers.
5. **TGF-beta 1 & C4a:** Inflammatory markers specific to biotoxin illness.
6. **VEGF (Vascular Endothelial Growth Factor):** Often suppressed in mold toxicity, starving tissues of oxygen.
