# The 30-Day Protocol

When you're hit with mold toxicity, the instinct is to kill the mold. This is a mistake. Your body is already overwhelmed; aggressively killing fungi releases more toxins and triggers Herxheimer reactions that can set you back months.

## Step 1: Stop the Exposure
You cannot heal in the environment that made you sick. Leave the house, or get proper remediation. Period.

## Step 2: Open the Drainage Pathways
Before binding toxins, the exits must be open.
- Hydration + Trace Minerals
- Sweating (Sauna)
- Bowel motility (Magnesium oxide/citrate)

## Step 3: Bind
Using targeted binders like Charcoal, Bentonite Clay, and Chlorella to grab mycotoxins in the gut before they recirculate.

## Step 4: System Support
Glutathione liposomal, NAD+ for cellular energy, and Phosphatidylcholine to repair cell membranes.
