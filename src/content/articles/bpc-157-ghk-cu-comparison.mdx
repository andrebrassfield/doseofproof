# BPC-157 and GHK-Cu

Peptides are the signaling molecules that tell your cells what to do. When dealing with chronic inflammation and tissue degradation, two stand out: BPC-157 and GHK-Cu.

## BPC-157 (Body Protection Compound)
Derived from human gastric juice, BPC-157 is the ultimate systemic healer. It promotes angiogenesis (new blood vessel formation), heals the gut lining, and rapidly repairs tendons and ligaments. For CCI and leaky gut, it's a foundational tool.

## GHK-Cu (Copper Peptide)
This naturally occurring copper complex drops significantly as we age. It resets genes to a younger, healthier state, promotes collagen synthesis, and has profound anti-inflammatory effects. It's the "remodeler" that cleans up the mess after the acute healing phase.

Used together, they form a synergistic repair protocol.
